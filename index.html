About Anthony McLean

Anthony McLean has spent over 20 years helping people move better, feel stronger, and recover from pain. What began as a passion for fitness evolved into a full wellness practice rooted in mobility, healing, and functional strength.

A personal trainer for two decades and a specialist in Thai massage, Hot Stone therapy, and FST stretching, Anthony is currently completing his RMT license (expected August 2026). His blended approach allows clients to receive customized training and therapeutic care under one roof.

As founder of ATM Mobility & Therapy, Anthony has created a welcoming space that supports clients of all backgrounds‚Äîespecially those who may feel overlooked in traditional fitness or clinical settings. His studio also houses multiple practitioners, giving clients access to a well-rounded team of wellness professionals.

Anthony's mission is simple: help people move without pain, improve their health, and feel supported at every stage of their journey.
